Action to be Taken

Firstly, the government should implement some laws to protect the customers. Meanwhile, the bank should be well-aware of the customers financial condition by updating more often and open a new customer service that will specifically focus on informing borrowers their money status and how they can avoid bankruptcy. As a result from this, banks might not be as pleasant as they are now since they may get less benefits from exploiting their customers when they can't pay the interest. However, they can definitely gain the customers' trust as they know that the bank that they are using is a good and thoughtful bank. Besides that, a boundary of investment such as the maximum possible amount of money for bank investment should also be set out for the investors. This will not only prevent the investors from going overboard but can also balance the economy of the country, minimizing the gap between people having different properties - no one will be too rich or too poor due to investing. Moreover, customers may ensure that they get the best privilege by raising their own awareness about their ability to pay for what they borrow, how the system works and is it actually good for them to lend that amount of money. By doing this together, all stakeholders will get a lot of benefits and the banking industry may become better and more trustworthy.
