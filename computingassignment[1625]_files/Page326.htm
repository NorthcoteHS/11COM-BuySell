On the other hand, banks would also face lots of problems, for example, robberies. Banks are always a target for robberies, especially online robbers, and they have to have a high security to prevent those things. Any serious robbery, for example, the bank's system has been hacked, could put a bank completely out of business and may be lead to bankruptcy. And if a bank was to go bankrupt, it would be put out of business forever. They also have to face the risk of depreciation, which is something drops a lot, for example currency, then banks would lose a significant amount of money. Another problem that the bank should be aware of is bitcoin or paypal. These services will simply allow customers to transfer or pay money without the use of banks, therefore banks would not get any profit from transfers.
