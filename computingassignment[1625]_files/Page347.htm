Investors

 

Being investors of the bank, they can get a lot of advantages from this issue as their profit relies on the banks, but also due to this, investors may also have some drawbacks. First of all, investors may make money from their investment to the bank from the percentage of income back. Besides that, by investing, they will also have the opportunity to improve their skills in doing deals, to meet the CEOs and improve their business skills and so on (Conville (2018). Steven Conville Report on Investment Banking. [online] Slideshare.net. Available at: https://www.slideshare.net/stevenconville/stevenconville [Accessed 18 Apr. 2018]. ). Also, to some of the investors, this can be their full-time employment, which means that this job has a very significant impact on their life. As a result, if the investment does not go well, all of the money that they have invested will be lost - their fate depends greatly on the bank.
