Despite being the target who suffer from the greatest damage in this issue, users and customers still get a lot of convenience using their banks' services. First of all, their money will be highly protected. Each bank has a security system to ensure their customers that their money is safe here. The maximum amount of money that can be insured is $100,000.( Consumer.westchestergov.com. (2018). Benefits of a Bank Account. [online] Available at: https://consumer.westchestergov.com/financial-education/money-management/benefits-of-a-bank-account  [Accessed 16 Apr. 2018]). Besides that when joining the bank, customers can also get some profit from the credit of the money that they put in their account. Moreover, when users need to pay for their education or to buy a new property, they can borrow an amount of money from the bank easily, as long as they have a good loan credit. In fact, people with good record can borrow money with a lower interest, which is good in a long run (Budgeting.thenest.com. (2018). Pros and cons of borrowing money from a financial institution. [online] Available at: https://budgeting.thenest.com/pros-cons-borrowing-money-financial-institution-22386.html  [Accessed 16 Apr. 2018].). All of these convenience and privilege have encouraged the users to make use of their privilege even more, which prevents them from realizing the situation when they go overboard.
