Nowadays, more and more people are using banks in order to gain heaps of convenience such as saving, protecting, borrowing and investing their money. Thanks to this convenience, they can get access to a better and more comfortable life. However, also due to this convenience, they may go overboard, struggle to control their finance and then finally end up with bankruptcy. This issue has great impacts on many stakeholders related to banking such as the customers and the bank themselves, the bank investors and even the government. Therefore, it's essential for all stakeholders to participate in tackling this problem.

Introduction to the Issue

Banks give people a better way of life. They allow people to keep their funds safe, undergo safe transactions and can help people who are not in a good financial position to borrow money so that they can pay back once they are in a better position to do so.
